# Hauling Safety Basics

## Pre-flight checklist

- Confirm contract collateral and destination station.
- Verify that the hauling ship is fit for the volume and route.
- Notify Dispatch of planned departure time.

## In-transit discipline

- Keep local and intel channels visible.
- Avoid unnecessary gate loitering.
- Use **instant dock** bookmarks for primary hubs.

## Docking etiquette

1. Dock immediately after landing on station grid.
2. Confirm delivery completion before undocking.
3. Report any discrepancies to Dispatch.

## Code of conduct

Use `safe_ping` in the operations channel when you need an escort.
